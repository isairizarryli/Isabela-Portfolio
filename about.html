Professional Background

I’m an Event Management major at the University of Central Florida with a passion for planning, logistics, and operations. I love the challenge of piecing together all the moving parts of an event to create a seamless, memorable experience. Whether coordinating vendors or developing layered backup plans, I thrive in environments where I can bring structure to complexity and make big ideas feel effortless.

My experience includes guest-facing and leadership roles at Walt Disney World and Kaplan Test Prep, along with serving as the Operations & Exhibition Services Lead for UCF’s Rosen College Career Fair. For that 500+ attendee event, I managed comprehensive logistics—including floorplans, signage, vendor load-in/load-out, and risk management. I also collaborated across departments to oversee registration, parking, guest experience, and real-time troubleshooting. I’m known for staying calm under pressure and stepping in as a dependable team lead when it matters most.

While working full-time, I’ve also maintained strong academic performance—earning Dean’s and President’s List honors. I approach every project with empathy, organization, and initiative, and I’m always looking for ways to contribute meaningfully while supporting those around me.

Beyond the Resume

I’m someone who leads with heart—nurturing, fair, and always showing up for the people (and animals) I care about. I find comfort in journaling and joy in quiet moments like movie marathons, game nights with loved ones, or wandering through theme parks with a good snack in hand.

Since middle school, I’ve rescued over 15 abandoned animals—what started as a dream to give every lonely pet a home has become a true calling. My most recent foster, a fragile kitten found at just a few days old, reminded me that even in uncertain moments, I’m capable of pushing forward with care and confidence.

Fostering has shaped my outlook in big ways. It’s given me resilience, empathy, and the ability to stay calm under pressure—all traits I carry with me in every event I help bring to life.
