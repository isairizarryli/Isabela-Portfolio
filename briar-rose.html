Inspiration & Concept

Briar Rosé was created as a final project for my Event Food & Beverage Strategies course, but the vision behind it came from years of loving the beauty in small details. Inspired by warm brunches, soft florals, and my love for helping others celebrate their story, Briar Rosé became a way for me to bring empathy and intention into catering. I imagined a brand that felt comforting yet elevated—something that blends heartfelt planning with elegant delivery.
